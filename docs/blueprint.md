# **App Name**: Siddhivinayak Collection

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Browsing: Browse a grid of sarees and silver items by category, with detailed product pages including large imagery, descriptions, and pricing. This covers product details for all specified pages (Shop, Product Page).
- Shopping Cart & Wishlist: Users can add/remove products, adjust quantities in a persistent shopping cart, and add items to a personal wishlist for later consideration.
- Secure Checkout Process: A multi-step checkout flow enabling users to enter shipping details, choose delivery options, and select from integrated payment methods like UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery via Razorpay/Stripe.
- Customer Account Management: Users can register, log in, view their order history, and manage their personal information. This also implies basic inventory management.
- Product Search & Filtering: On the shop page, users can search for products and filter by attributes such as Price, Color, Material, and Occasion, as well as sort by Popularity, Price (low-high), and New Arrivals.
- Responsive & SEO Optimization: The entire website design is optimized for mobile-first responsiveness across all devices, with fundamental SEO elements integrated for search engine visibility on terms like 'Buy Sarees Online India'.
- AI Styling & Presentation Tool: An AI tool that suggests elegant saree draping styles or optimal display arrangements for silver gifting items, offering personalized visual recommendations based on product attributes and occasion.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: A refined, soft off-white cream (#FBFBF9) chosen for a luxurious, minimal base that ensures visual elegance without being stark white.
- Primary accent for text and UI elements: Deep, rich black (#000000), providing strong contrast and sophistication for headlines and critical information.
- Highlight and interactive elements: Deep Royal Maroon (#83162A), used sparingly for Call-to-Action buttons, small accents, and active states to convey luxury and a subtle Indian heritage.
- Unified font family: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for all headings and body text, ensuring a modern, clean, and highly legible experience consistent with a minimal, high-end aesthetic. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Typography style: Bold, clean headings and elegant, minimal body text will be employed, emphasizing a strong visual hierarchy across all content.
- Iconography: Minimalistic, crisp line-art style icons with sharp 90° edges, mirroring the overall geometric design language and contributing to a clutter-free interface.
- Grid-based layout: A clean, disciplined grid structure with strict adherence to 90° angles and no rounded corners on any UI elements. Generous, consistent margins and padding will define spacious sections and visual breathing room.
- Animations & Transitions: Subtle hover effects, smooth yet quick transitions, and minimal animations are implemented to enhance interactivity without distracting from the premium, understated design.
